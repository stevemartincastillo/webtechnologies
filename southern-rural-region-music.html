Music:

Many of the rhythms and melodies that fuels the mayan people can be heard annually during the observation of the Deer Dance festival. This vibrant dance is much more than a performance—it’s a ritual of renewal that honors the sacred deer, a symbol of agility, abundance, and the spirit of the forest. By celebrating the deer dance, the community reaffirms its deep connection with nature and its ancestral traditions, ensuring that these precious cultural values are passed on to future generations. Instrument typically used in playing these melodies is:

  • Marimba
