Music:

In the Central region the beats of Soca and calypso rhythms are the preferred genres. These vibrant rhythms fill the air during Belize City's lively parades every September, creating an atmosphere of pure jubilation. Let the music lift your spirits and carry you along on this unforgettable journey!
Instruments typically used in playing these genres are:

  1. the guitar
  2. various wind instruments like the trumpet, trombone and saxophone
  3. drums
  4. steel pan
