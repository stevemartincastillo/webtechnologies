Brief History:

Welcome to the northern region of Belize, where the spirit of adventure and history fly high.
Back in 1848, brave Mestizo families from Yucatán, Mexico, left behind a time of conflict during the Caste War. They set off on a daring journey after a major defeat from the Yucatec Mayas, seeking peace and a fresh start in a new land. Their arrival in our region—especially in places like Corozal and Orange Walk—bringing with them a vibrant culture and traditions that continue to color our everyday life. Today, the Mestizo community is a proud and lively part of the northern region, sharing in a rich heritage that shines through in their music, celebrations, and daily rhythms.
