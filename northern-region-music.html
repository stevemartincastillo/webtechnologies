Music:

In the northern region the beats of Ranchera and Jarana Yucateca set the tone, echoing the rich heritage of the Mestizo people, who are mixture of Mexican Yucatec Mayas and Spaniards. Instruments typically used in playing these genres are:

  1. the guitar,
  2. various wind instruments like the trumpet, trombone and saxophone
  3. and drums
