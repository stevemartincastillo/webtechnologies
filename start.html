How the Game Works

Explore Belize:
  • Travel through an interactive map of Belize as I share fascinating stories about each region’s unique musical traditions—from the mestizo influences in the north to the soulful sounds of the Garifuna in the south.
  • Learn & Play: After each regional story, you’ll face a short trivia challenge. Answer questions about what you’ve learned to earn badges along the way.
  • Progress with Fun: To continue your journey, you must score at least 70% on the trivia. Don’t worry if you need to try again—you can review and retake the quiz until you’re ready to move on!
  • Earn Your Title: Complete all regions and collect all badges to be crowned the ultimate Belize Music Master.
